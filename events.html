Events Attended

Google Cloud Africa Region Launch

March 19, 2025 Gallager Conference Centre, Midrand, South Africa

Attending the africa-south1 Google Cloud Region launch in Johannesburg, South Africa was an electrifying experience! Witnessing this milestone reinforced how innovation continues to push boundaries. With Google Cloud's decades-long AI and data leadership—plus a $30B investment in Africa—the future of cloud computing is full of opportunities.

Highlights:

  • AgentSpace – A powerful tool for connecting data sources and building AI agents to automate workflows.
  • Notebook LM – A smart research tool that simplifies document analysis with AI-generated summaries, including podcast-style audio overviews.

Build with AI Johannesburg – A Hands-On AI Experience

March 21, 2025 Johannesburg, South Africa

I attended the Build with AI event, hosted by Google Developer Group (GDG) Johannesburg. This full-day event was packed with networking, insightful talks, and coding workshops where we explored Gemini in AI Studio & Vertex AI and Vertex AI Flutter app integrations.

Highlights:

  • Intelligent Loan Agent – Built using Google AI Studio to simulate predictive lending with a 5% monthly interest rate.
  • Automated Product Catalog for Small Businesses – A zero-cost solution using Google Sheets, Vercel, and Google Cloud to streamline WhatsApp Business catalog updates.

Global Power Platform Bootcamp

March 1, 2025 Microsoft Bryanston, South Africa

On March 1st, I had the incredible opportunity to attend the Global Power Platform Bootcamp at Microsoft Bryanston, South Africa—a day packed with insights, innovation, and inspiring conversations. My focus was Power BI & Microsoft Fabric!

Highlights:

  • Security in Power BI and Fabric - Michael Johnson
  • Drive Action with Insights using the Power Platform & Fabric Real-Time Intelligence – Sven Vosse
  • Power BI Magic in Power Apps – Carike Botha
  • Putting the 'F' in Fiscal: Fun & Functional Power BI Financial Dashboards – Niren Gooroocharan
