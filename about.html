Biography

Gcinithemba Sherilyn Maphosa is a Microsoft Certified Azure Data Scientist and data analyst from Zimbabwe with a BSc Hons in Computer Science and over five years of experience in data analytics and business intelligence. She is the founder of Data Wired, a consultancy and training company that empowers professionals and organizations with data-driven skills and strategic decision-making across sectors.

Committed to gender equity in tech, she leads DataXplore, an initiative providing mentorship, community, and free access to premium learning for women in data. Gcinithemba has led mentorship programs and webinars supporting women in data science across Zimbabwe and South Africa and serves on the board of Elevate Trust, an organization advancing STEM innovation among youth.

She is always looking to deepen her expertise in data science, expand her knowledge of cloud infrastructure and AI agents, strengthen her leadership capacity, and build collaborations that drive inclusive, scalable impact across Africa's data ecosystems.
